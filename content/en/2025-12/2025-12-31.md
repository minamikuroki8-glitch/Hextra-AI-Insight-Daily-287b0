### Cutting-edge Research

1.  **New method enables large models to peer-review each other.**
    The LLM-PeerReview integrated framework has been proposed. This new method allows large models to peer-review each other, simulating a peer review mechanism to select the best responses. It significantly boosts effectiveness without human supervision and leverages collective intelligence to outperform the existing [Smoothie model (AI News)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.23213). This offers a fresh approach for models to self-evolve.

2.  **Visual recognition PanCAN network sees new breakthrough.**
    The visual recognition field welcomes a breakthrough with the PanCAN network. This network aggregates multi-order geometric contextual information, tackling the problem of overlooked complex relationships between objects. It dramatically improves multi-label classification accuracy, leading to more precise image interpretation. It has shown [excellent performance (AI News)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.23486) on datasets like COCO.

3.  **Agent AI proactively safeguards the software supply chain.**
    Agent AI is building a proactive defense system to safeguard the software supply chain. Combining large model reasoning with reinforcement learning, it automatically discovers and patches vulnerabilities in production environments. This system is way more efficient and reacts lightning-fast compared to traditional passive defenses.
