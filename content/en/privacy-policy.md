### Tracking Tech & Cookies
Cookies and similar tracking tech? We're all about 'em! We use them to keep tabs on activity on our Service and squirrel away certain info. The tracking tech we use includes beacons, tags, and scripts, all designed to gather and track info, plus boost and analyze our Service. The tech we might use includes:

Heads up, though: if you don't play nice with Cookies, you might miss out on some parts of our Service. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to wave goodbye to Cookies, our Service might just use 'em.
- **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and our emails might have tiny electronic files called Web Beacons (also known as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These little guys let the Company (for example) count users who've visited those pages or opened an email, and for other juicy website stats (like tracking how popular a certain section is and making sure the system and server are running smoothly).

Cookies can be either "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. When you're offline, Persistent Cookies stick around on your personal computer or mobile device. Session Cookies, on the other hand, vanish the moment you slam shut your web browser. üí®

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies, and here's why:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are super important for hooking you up with services available through the Website and letting you use some of its cool features. They help verify user identity and prevent sketchy use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for can't be delivered, and we only use these Cookies to, well, deliver those services to you!

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are used to check if users have given a thumbs-up to the use of Cookies on the Website. üëç

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices you make when you're cruising around the Website, like remembering your login deets or language preferences. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience, saving you from having to re-enter your preferences every single time you use the Website. Sweet!
